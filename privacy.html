Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Log My Golf ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use logmygolf.com and our mobile apps (together, the "Service").

1. Who we are

The Service is operated by James Haken, based in the United Kingdom. For any privacy questions, or to request access to or deletion of your data, contact us at support@logmygolf.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: your email address and password (passwords are stored hashed by our authentication provider — we never see them).
  • Profile data: display name, username, optional avatar image, home course and any details you add.
  • Golf data: rounds, scores, handicap, courses played, reviews and related statistics you record.
  • Social data: follows, likes, and other golfers you interact with on the Service.
  • Photos: images you choose to upload (e.g. avatars or course photos).
  • Technical data: basic device and usage information collected automatically to keep the Service running securely.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the Service — store and display your rounds, scores and handicap.
  • To enable social features such as following other golfers and leaderboards.
  • To secure accounts and prevent abuse.
  • To improve and maintain the Service.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Where your data is stored

Data is stored using Supabase, our database and authentication provider, on servers located in the EU (eu-west-1). Some technical processing may occur in other regions where our infrastructure providers operate.

5. Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the Service (e.g. Supabase for hosting/auth, and our website host). Some information you add — such as your display name, avatar, and rounds — is visible to other users through social and leaderboard features. We may disclose data if required by law.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account and associated data at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete account, or by emailing us. Deletion is permanent.

7. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data and golf records, except where we are legally required to retain limited information.

8. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

Questions? Email support@logmygolf.com.
